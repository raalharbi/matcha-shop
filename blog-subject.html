Iced Lavender Cream Oatmilk Matcha Latte

12/5/2024

The change of the season brings new matcha latte creations! You might have noticed the new trendy iced lavender matcha lattes making waves in coffee shops. But guess what? We've got an equally delicious recipe that you can whip up at home, saving you both money and the hassle of waiting in line. The combination of floral and aromatic lavender, subtle sweetness of vanilla and earthy matcha notes delivers an elegant and flavorful latte.

Ingredient Quantity
Jade Leaf Sweetened Matcha Latte Mix 2-3 tsp.
Warm water 2 oz
Milk 1 cup
Ice -
Heavy whipping cream 3 tbsp.
2% milk 2 tbsp.
Lavender syrup 1 tbsp.
Vanilla syrup 1/2 tbsp.
Optional: Food coloring or blackcurrant powder -
Topping: Organic Lavender Flowers -
